# 🌱 Biogrix

### Biogas Plant Management & Utility Billing System

Biogrix is a full-stack platform designed to digitize the management of community-level biogas energy systems. It enables plant operators to manage biogas plants, monitor gas distribution, track household consumption, automate billing, and streamline maintenance operations through a centralized dashboard.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🏭 Biogas Plant Management
* 👥 Customer & Household Management
* 📊 Real-Time Gas Usage Tracking
* 📟 Gas Meter Monitoring
* 💳 Automated Billing & Payments
* 🛠️ Complaint & Maintenance Management
* 📈 Reports & Analytics Dashboard
* 🔐 Secure Authentication & Role Management
